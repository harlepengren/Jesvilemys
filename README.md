A quick and replayable game about fighting! Expertly run around, jump, and possibly dash to gain an edge over other players; All in the faith of winning! Every few minutes, all the item's *souls* will swap places. Places of safety and sanity reverse as players and items change tactics.

# Item Souls
## What are *item souls*?
Item souls are what items *do*. Item souls can swap *bodies*, which means that an item that was a bomb before may act like a star!

## Catalog of Souls
- None - Nothing happens.
- Explode - Launches the player into the air, like fireworks!
- Freeze - Locks the player in place- still allows them to punch.
- Faster - Faster movement, faster jumps.
